FAQs

  • An Account Statement (AS) is a consolidated document that provides a detailed summary of all mutual fund transactions carried out by an investor. It includes information such as purchases, redemptions, switches, dividends, and the units allotted in the folio.

  • You can download the AS as soon as you begin investing with us.
    An Account Statement is generated each time a transaction is processed and units are allotted in the investor’s folio.

  • No. There is no charge or fee for receiving or downloading your Account Statement.

  • You can download the following types of statements:

    PAN-based Account Statement: Consolidated view of all folios linked to the PAN.
    Folio-based Account Statement: Statement specific to a single folio.
